Park Rides and Attractions

Peach State Plunge

This fast roller coaster twists, loops, and dives through sharp turns. It is the perfect ride for guests who want a high-energy thrill experience.

Southern SkyWheel

This giant wheel lifts guests high above the park so they can enjoy a wide view of Georgia Southern Studios from the sky.

Savannah Swing Carousel

This colorful swing ride spins riders through the air while bright lights and music create a classic carnival feeling.

Southern Splash River

This splash-filled river adventure sends guests through rapids and surprise bursts of water for a refreshing ride on warm Georgia days.

Dash Car Racers

This go-kart attraction lets friends and families race around curves and straightaways in a fun competition for first place.
