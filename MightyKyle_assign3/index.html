About Us

Georgia Southern Studios is a fun-filled amusement park in southern Georgia with thrill rides, family attractions, tasty food, and special events throughout the year. The park mixes exciting adventures with a welcoming Southern atmosphere, which makes it a favorite destination for both students and families.
